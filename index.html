I am a Senior Energy and Business Transformation Professional with over 25 years spanning Indian Oil Corporation Limited (IOCL) and IBP Co. Ltd — from engineering infrastructure at Haldia's USD 28M oil terminal to leading LPG commercial strategy across India as Business General Manager.

My career has been defined by operating at scale, in regulated environments, with accountability for teams, P&L, and policy. As Chief LPG Manager for one of the states (Jharkhand) , I led a team of professionals across 390+ distributors and served as State Nodal Officer for PM Ujjwala Yojana — India's landmark clean-cooking program — from 2016 to 2019.

Parallel to my executive career, I have built deep expertise in AI, Machine Learning, and Business Analytics through my MS at the University of Arizona's Eller College of Management (Grade: A, graduating May 2026). My KPMG US capstone — a GLP-1 Market Prioritization Framework — combined K-means clustering on 33,000+ ZCTAs with federal CDC, CMS, ACS, and HRSA datasets to deliver an interactive HTML decision tool.

I have deployed two live machine learning web applications, built an AI Resume Optimizer achieving 92%+ ATS match rates, and work fluently across Python, R, SQL, TensorFlow, XGBoost, Tableau, and GenAI/GPT-4o stacks. I bring the rare combination of C-suite operational credibility and hands-on AI engineering to every engagement.
